About RD Dance Academy

Welcome to RD Dance Academy, where we nurture the talent of aspiring dancers. Our academy offers a variety of dance forms taught by experienced instructors. Join us to learn and master your favorite dance styles!

RD Dance Academy was founded in 2010 and has been inspiring dancers for over a decade.

Dance Forms We Teach

Ballet

Classical ballet training focusing on technique, grace, and poise.

Hip Hop

Energetic and street-style dance form with a focus on rhythm and improvisation.

Contemporary

Modern dance style that blends elements of ballet, jazz, and other genres.

Salsa

Latin dance known for its vibrant and rhythmic movements.

Jazz

Dynamic dance form that incorporates elements of ballet and modern dance.

Tap

Dance style characterized by rhythmical tapping of the feet.
